OUR MISSION

In every dogs life, their face gets covered in powdered sugar as the reach their golden years. Here at PSAS we ensure those years are truly golden, giving haven to those who don't have a place to call home. Thanks to the many donations and supporters, we have been able to give these dogs the best care in their old age.
With their advanced age and special care, Senior dogs can be a big commitment that not everyone is prepared for. We work to not only make sure our residents are ready for their new home, but also to make sure their home is ready for them too! PSAS provides resources to new pet owners and access to information to better care for their new powdered friend.
